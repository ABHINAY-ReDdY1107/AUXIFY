FAQ

Common questions, clear answers.

What is the typical project timeline?

Most projects take 2 to 4 weeks depending on scope. A simple website or automation workflow can ship in under 2 weeks, while larger product builds with multiple screens and integrations typically need 3 to 4 weeks.

How does pricing work?

Pricing is project-based, not hourly. After an initial conversation to understand scope, you receive a clear quote with deliverables, timeline, and revision rounds included. No surprise fees.

Can you work with existing designs or codebases?

Absolutely. Auxify can pick up from Figma files, existing front-end code, or brand guidelines. If you already have a design system, the build integrates with it rather than starting from scratch.

What tools and technologies do you use?

The stack depends on the project. Web builds use HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like React or Next.js. Automation uses N8N and Python. Design work happens in Figma and Framer. AI workflows leverage modern LLM APIs and structured data pipelines.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance?

Yes. After launch, Auxify offers optional maintenance plans for content updates, bug fixes, performance monitoring, and feature additions. You also receive clean handoff documentation so your team can manage things independently.
